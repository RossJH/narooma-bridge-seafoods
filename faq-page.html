FAQ!

Some of the things our frequent customers want to know.


Where to park? : See our parking page for more details.


Prices? : Due to the fishing market constantly changing we arent able to display set pricing on all items. Please call us before coming in if you would like to check first.


Seafood availability? : Due to the seasonality of fishing we arent able to stock all items year round. Please call us before coming in if you would like to check first.


Do we wholesale? : Yes we do. Please call us to discuss pricing and quantity. Keep in mind this will be based on time of year and available quota.


Are we fully licensed? : Yes we hold various fishing licenses in NSW.


Can we dine in? : No we arent a restaurant / co-op and dont have an indoor dining area.


Do we fish sustainably? : Due to fishing licenses we are heavy regulated with fishing quota to meet sustainability to make sure the Eurobodalla ocean waters will thrive for years to come.


Do you offer fish and chips or hot takeaway food? : Unfortunately no we arent offering cooked fish and chips at this stage. come.
