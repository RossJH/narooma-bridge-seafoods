Parking can be crowded during holidays.

Please see below the 4 closes and main carparks.

Narooma Bridge Seafood Carpark

If you are heading south on the princess hwy to pull in on the left.


If you are heading north it is double lined so we dont recommend crossing them.


Riverview Road

If you are heading North on the princess hwy when you come to the end of the bridge turn left onto Riverview road. Then pull in on the left straight away, you will turn down underneath the bridge to a small carpark.


Once parked you are able to walk underneath the bridge on the new boardwalk and come up right next to our shop.


Caravan Park

If you are heading south on the princess hwy and missed our main carpark. Head over the bridge and pull in on the left.


There is a small carpark and also restrooms here. You will need to walk back over the bridge along the footpath.


Mill Bay Boatramp

If you are heading south on the scenic route past the boardwalk you can park at the boatramp. You will need to walk over the hill and down the path to the boat shed.
