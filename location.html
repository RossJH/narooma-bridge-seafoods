Parking!

There are 4 places to park including the main carpark during busy times.


Main carpark : If you are heading south on the princess hwy to pull in on the left.


Riverview Road : If you are heading North on the princess hwy to pull in on the left and then left again.


Bridge Boatramp : If you are heading north on the princess hwy to pull in on the left. You will need to cross the highway and walk over the bridge.


Caravan Park : If you are heading south on the princess hwy and missed the main carpark. Head over the bridge and pull in on the left. There are also restrooms here. You will need to walk back over the bridge.


Mill Bay Boatramp : If you are heading south on the scenic route you can park at the boatramp. You will need to walk over the hill and down the path to the boat shed.
